About Us

Welcome to the Farmers International Development Agency (FIDA)! FIDA is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable agricultural development and reducing poverty in developing countries.

Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers to improve their livelihoods, reduce food insecurity, and build resilient communities. we are mostly working with local co-operatives, youth groups, women clubs among them to ensure that together we promote the fight against poverty, malnutrition, HIV and transform these powerhouse clubs, NGOs into entrepreneurship hubs.

  • At FIDA, we believe that sustainable agriculture is key to reducing poverty and building resilient communities
  • We believe investing in rural people is a long-term solution to so many of the problems we face today. Hunger, poverty, youth unemployment and forced migration – all have deep roots in rural areas; and all can be vastly improved through investing in small-scale agriculture and inclusive rural development.
  • Currently as FIDA we are running Poultry management programs among our programs which also includes feed formulation training for groups so as to cut costs of production by 50 percent as the cost of doing business in livestock farming comes from stockfeed. so our core principle in agriculture is value addition for all our partners.

Thank you for visiting our website. Please explore our site to learn more about our work, and how you can support our mission to promote sustainable agricultural development and reduce poverty.

Services

Agricultural Training and Technical Assistance

The Farmers International Development Agency (FIDA) provides training and technical assistance to smallholder farmers in developing countries to improve their farming practices. FIDA's experts work with farmers to promote sustainable agriculture practices such as crop diversification, soil conservation, and water management. The training provided by FIDA aims to increase the productivity and resilience of smallholder farmers, and thereby improving food security and reducing poverty.

Research and Knowledge Sharing

FIDA conducts research and knowledge-sharing activities to inform and improve agricultural development strategies and policies. FIDA's experts research and analyze trends in agricultural development, and share their findings with policymakers, other organizations, and farmers. This helps to promote evidence-based policies and approaches to agricultural development, and to build the capacity of smallholder farmers to participate in the development process.

Market Development

FIDA also provides support to farmers in developing countries to access markets for their products. FIDA's experts work with farmers to identify market opportunities, develop value chains, and improve access to finance. This support helps smallholder farmers to sell their products at fair prices, increasing their income and improving their livelihoods.

A heathy farmer a heathy nation

The Healthy Farmer, Healthy Nation program is a flagship initiative of the Farmers International Development Agency (FIDA) aimed at promoting the health and well-being of smallholder farmers in developing countries. The program recognizes that the health of farmers is critical to the success of agricultural development and poverty reduction efforts, as healthy farmers are better able to work productively and take care of their families.

Youth programs on Environmental Sustainability and Environmental protection

The Youth Programs on Environmental Sustainability is a key initiative of the Farmers International Development Agency (FIDA) aimed at engaging and empowering young people to take action on environmental sustainability. The program recognizes that young people are critical to creating a sustainable future, and seeks to build their capacity to lead and participate in environmental initiatives.

Feed the Nation Feed Africa

The Feed the Nation, Feed Africa program is a flagship initiative of the Farmers International Development Agency (FIDA) aimed at increasing agricultural productivity and food security in Africa. The program recognizes the critical role of agriculture in reducing poverty, improving nutrition, and promoting economic growth, and seeks to support smallholder farmers to become more productive and sustainable.
